The Gospel of John has inspired seekers, saints, doubters, and believers for centuries. Filled with images of light and darkness, water and wine, bread and vineyards, it invites us beyond surface understanding and into a deeper awareness of Divine Presence.

*Light Unending* offers 90 contemplative reflections inspired by John's Gospel, each written to help you slow down, listen deeply, and encounter the spiritual realities hidden within ordinary life. Rather than a verse-by-verse study, these meditations explore themes of love, grace, belonging, healing, and transformation through the lens of quiet contemplation.

Each reflection concludes with a simple breath prayer, creating space to carry the day's insight into moments of stillness, work, prayer, and daily living. Whether read in the morning, before bed, or during a quiet pause in the day, these reflections are designed to be companions on the journey rather than lessons to master.

There is no rush. Read slowly. Sit with a passage that speaks to you. Return to a prayer that stirs your heart. The invitation of the Beloved remains what it has always been: Come and see.
