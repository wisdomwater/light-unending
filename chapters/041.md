## 41 — The Source Within

> *"I know him, because I am from him..."* — John 7:29

Many of us spend years trying to discover who we are. We gather accomplishments, roles, beliefs, successes, failures, and identities, hoping that somewhere among them we will find a stable sense of self. Yet even after acquiring many things, a quiet question often remains: What is the deepest truth about me?

Beneath all the labels we carry is a mystery that cannot be reduced to a résumé, a personality type, or a life story. At our deepest level, we arise from a sacred source. We are not self-created. We are expressions of a life that was here before us and will remain long after us. The breath moving through us in this moment is itself a gift.

Much of our suffering comes from forgetting this. We begin to believe we are isolated beings struggling alone in an indifferent universe. We define ourselves by our wounds, our fears, or the expectations placed upon us by others. The mind builds countless stories about separation, and before long we feel disconnected from the very life that sustains us.

The spiritual journey is not primarily about becoming something new. It is about remembering what has always been true. It is a gradual awakening to the reality that we belong to God more deeply than we belong to any fear, failure, or false identity. The source of life is not far away. It is closer than our next breath, quietly holding us even when we have forgotten how to trust.

As this awareness grows, something within us begins to relax. We no longer need to prove our worth through endless striving. We no longer need to defend a fragile sense of self. We can rest in the knowledge that our value does not come from what we achieve, but from the love from which we came and the love in which we continue to live.

Perhaps today is an invitation to return to that deeper center. Beneath every role, beneath every worry, beneath every story you tell about yourself, there is a quiet place where divine life is already present. Sit there for a moment. Listen. You may discover that what you have been seeking is the very source from which you have always come.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I come from Love*
>
> Exhale: *I rest in Love*
