## 27 — The Healing Power of Trust

> *"The man believed the word that Jesus spoke to him, and he went his way."* — John 4:50

There are moments in life when certainty is unavailable.

We long for guarantees, visible evidence, and clear outcomes before we take the next step. We want to know that everything will work out before we entrust ourselves to the journey. Yet much of life unfolds in a space where complete certainty cannot be found. We are invited to move forward without seeing the entire path.

Trust begins where control reaches its limits.

This kind of trust is not blind denial of reality. It does not ignore pain, loss, or uncertainty. Rather, it is a quiet willingness to place our lives in hands larger than our own understanding. It is the courage to believe that love is still at work even when we cannot yet see the results.

Often the deepest healing begins before we recognize it. Something shifts within us when we stop clinging so tightly to outcomes. Anxiety softens. Fear loosens its grip. The heart becomes more spacious. We discover that trust itself has a healing quality, not because it changes every circumstance immediately, but because it changes the way we carry those circumstances.

Many of us spend enormous energy trying to manage every possibility. We rehearse future conversations, anticipate problems, and attempt to protect ourselves from disappointment. Yet the soul cannot find peace through endless control. Peace grows when we learn to rest in the Presence that accompanies us through every uncertainty.

This does not mean we become passive. We still act, love, serve, plan, and respond. But our actions arise from a deeper foundation. Instead of being driven primarily by fear, we begin to move from trust. We discover that faith is less about having all the answers and more about remaining open to the One who walks with us through every question.

The path ahead may not always be visible, but we are not walking it alone. Beneath every uncertainty flows a deeper faithfulness. We take the next step, then the next, trusting that the Love which called us forward is already present in the road ahead.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I place my trust in You*
>
> Exhale: *Lead me one step further*
