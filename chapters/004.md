## 4 — The Life Hidden in All Things

> *"The light shines in the darkness."* — John 1:5

There are seasons when life feels ordinary and predictable. We move through familiar routines, attend to daily responsibilities, and often overlook the quiet miracle surrounding us. Yet beneath the surface of every ordinary moment, a deeper life is present. The same mystery that causes seeds to awaken in spring, rivers to flow toward the sea, and stars to burn in distant galaxies is continually at work within us.

We often think of life as something we possess, something measured by years, health, accomplishments, or activity. But the deepest life cannot be reduced to any of these things. It is the sacred energy of being itself—the divine breath that sustains every creature, the unseen current that holds creation together moment by moment.

Sometimes we become disconnected from this deeper life. Anxiety narrows our vision. Grief clouds our awareness. Busyness scatters our attention. We begin to move through our days as though we are separate and alone, carrying the weight of existence upon our own shoulders. Yet even in those moments, the hidden life remains present. We may lose awareness of it, but it never loses hold of us.

When we become still enough to notice, signs of this life appear everywhere. We see it in acts of kindness that ask for nothing in return. We feel it in moments of genuine connection. We encounter it in beauty, creativity, forgiveness, and compassion. Something within us recognizes these moments because they resonate with the deepest truth of our own being.

The spiritual journey is not about escaping the world in order to find life elsewhere. It is about awakening to the sacred life already pulsing through the world around us and within us. The divine presence is not confined to extraordinary experiences. It shines through ordinary existence, quietly inviting us to participate more fully in its abundance.

As we learn to trust this hidden life, fear begins to loosen its grip. We discover that we are not isolated fragments struggling to survive in a disconnected universe. We are part of a living whole, sustained by a Love that continually gives itself away. Beneath every breath, beneath every moment, beneath every living thing, that Life continues to flow.
