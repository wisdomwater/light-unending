## 17 — Born from Above

> *"You must be born anew."* — John 3:7

There are moments in life when we discover that information alone cannot transform us. We can accumulate knowledge, beliefs, accomplishments, and experiences, yet still feel as though something essential remains untouched. The deepest changes often arrive not as new ideas but as a new way of seeing.

To be born from above is not merely to adopt a different set of thoughts. It is to awaken to a deeper reality that has always been present beneath the surface of ordinary life. It is the gradual realization that we are more than our fears, more than our roles, more than the stories we tell about ourselves. Something within us longs for a larger life, a life rooted in Spirit rather than driven by anxiety, striving, or self-protection.

This awakening rarely happens all at once. Like the wind, it moves in ways we cannot fully predict or control. Sometimes it arrives through joy. Sometimes through loss. Sometimes through a quiet moment when the heart suddenly recognizes a truth it has always known. We cannot manufacture this movement, but we can learn to welcome it.

Many of us spend years trying to improve ourselves when what we truly need is a deeper surrender. The invitation is not to become someone else but to allow the false and fearful layers of identity to loosen their grip. Beneath them is a life already connected to the Source from which it came, already held within a love greater than it can comprehend.

Every genuine spiritual journey contains this mystery of new birth. Again and again, we are invited to release what is small so that something larger can emerge. The old self contracts around fear. The new self expands into trust. The old self clings to certainty. The new self learns to live within wonder.

The breath of God continues to move through the world and through every human heart. Wherever love awakens, wherever compassion grows, wherever light breaks through old patterns of separation, new birth is already taking place.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Awaken me to Your life*
>
> Exhale: *Breathe through me*
