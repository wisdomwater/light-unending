## 39 — Rivers of Living Water

> *"From within him will flow rivers of living water."* — John 7:38

Many of us spend our lives searching for what will finally satisfy the deep thirst within. We seek fulfillment in achievement, relationships, possessions, knowledge, certainty, or recognition. Some of these things are beautiful gifts, yet none of them can fully quiet the longing that lives beneath every other desire.

There is a deeper thirst hidden within the human heart. It is the longing to belong, to be loved, to know that our lives matter, and to feel connected to the source of life itself. Often we imagine that what we need is somewhere outside of us, waiting to be discovered if only we search hard enough. Yet the spiritual journey slowly reveals a surprising truth: what we seek is already seeking us.

The living presence of God is not a distant reward reserved for the spiritually accomplished. It is a spring already flowing beneath the surface of our lives. Most of the time we are simply unaware of it. Like someone standing beside a river while dying of thirst, we often overlook the grace that surrounds and sustains us in every moment.

As we learn to trust this deeper presence, something begins to change. We stop grasping so tightly. We stop trying to manufacture meaning through endless effort. Instead of living from scarcity, we begin to live from abundance. The love we receive naturally becomes love we share. The peace we discover naturally extends toward others.

The image is not merely of a person drinking, but of a person becoming a source of refreshment. Divine life is never meant to stop with us. As love fills the heart, it overflows. Kindness becomes more natural. Compassion becomes more instinctive. The river that heals us becomes the river that heals the world around us.

Perhaps today the invitation is simply to become still enough to notice the water already flowing. Beneath the noise, beneath the striving, beneath the fear, there is a quiet stream of grace that has never ceased its movement. We do not have to create it. We need only learn to drink.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Living water within me*
>
> Exhale: *Flow through my life*
