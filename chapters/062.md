## 62 — Let Yourself Be Loved

> *"You don’t know what I am doing now, but you will understand later."* — John 13:7

There are seasons when life feels confusing. We cannot see where the path is leading. Events unfold in ways we would never have chosen, and the meaning remains hidden from us. We search for answers, explanations, and certainty, yet clarity does not immediately come.

In those moments, the deepest invitation may not be to understand, but to trust. We often assume that peace comes after understanding. Yet much of the spiritual journey unfolds in the opposite direction. First comes surrender. First comes openness. Understanding, if it comes at all, often arrives much later.

Love is frequently at work beneath the surface of our lives long before we recognize it. Quiet transformations are taking place in hidden places. Old wounds are softening. New capacities are emerging. The soul is being shaped by experiences that seem ordinary or even painful while they are happening.

This is why we do not need to have everything figured out before moving forward. We do not need complete certainty before opening our hearts. The Light is not waiting at the end of the journey. The Light is already present within the journey itself, guiding us through every question and every mystery.

Perhaps the hardest lesson of all is learning to stop resisting the love that is being offered to us. We want explanations. We want guarantees. Yet often the sacred invitation is much simpler: let yourself be cared for. Let yourself be held. Let yourself be loved, even before you understand why.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I open my heart*
>
> Exhale: *To the love that holds me*
