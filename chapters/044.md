## 44 — The Freedom of Truth

> *"You will know the truth, and the truth will make you free."* — John 8:32

Many people think of freedom as the ability to do whatever they wish. Yet experience often teaches otherwise. We can be surrounded by choices and still feel trapped. We can possess comfort, success, or approval and still find ourselves bound by fear, anxiety, resentment, or the need to prove our worth.

The deepest forms of bondage are often invisible. We become attached to old stories about who we are. We carry wounds that quietly shape our reactions. We cling to identities, opinions, and assumptions because they feel familiar. Without realizing it, we begin living inside a small room built from our own perceptions.

Truth opens a window.

Not merely facts or ideas, but the deeper truth of reality itself. The truth that we are loved before we achieve. The truth that our worth is not something we must earn. The truth that beneath our fears and defenses there remains a deeper self that has never been separated from the Source of Life.

As this truth begins to take root, something within us relaxes. We no longer need to cling so tightly to being right. We no longer need to hide our weaknesses or defend every part of our self-image. The light gently reveals what is real, and what is real becomes the foundation for genuine freedom.

This freedom is not the absence of responsibility. It is the absence of illusion. It is the freedom that comes from seeing clearly and living honestly. It is the freedom of a heart that no longer needs to run from itself because it has discovered that divine love has been present all along.

The journey of awakening is not about acquiring something new. It is about seeing what has always been true. And each glimpse of that truth loosens another chain, opens another door, and invites us into a larger life.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Open me to truth*
>
> Exhale: *Lead me into freedom*
