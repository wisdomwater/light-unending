## 13 — Heaven Standing Open

> *“You will see heaven opened.”* — John 1:51

There are moments when life feels divided. We separate the sacred from the ordinary, the spiritual from the physical, heaven from earth. We imagine that God exists somewhere beyond our reach, hidden behind a veil that only a few can cross. The result is often a quiet loneliness, as though we are living at a distance from the very Presence we long to know.

Yet the deepest spiritual awakenings often reveal something entirely different. They do not show us a God who has suddenly arrived. They reveal a Presence that was already here. What changes is not the distance between heaven and earth, but our ability to perceive the connection that has always existed.

There are brief moments when this becomes unmistakably clear. A sunset fills us with wonder. A piece of music opens something deep within the heart. A conversation leaves us feeling profoundly seen. A season of prayer brings an unexpected stillness. For an instant, the world appears luminous and alive. We sense that reality is far more sacred than we normally allow ourselves to believe.

These moments are gifts, but they are also invitations. They encourage us to look beyond the assumption that the Divine is absent. The spiritual journey is not about escaping the world to find God elsewhere. It is about awakening to the sacred depth hidden within this very life. Heaven is not merely a future destination. It is a reality that continually touches and permeates the present moment.

As our awareness deepens, we begin to notice countless places where grace is moving. Acts of kindness become signs of a greater love. Forgiveness becomes a doorway into freedom. Compassion reveals the interconnectedness of all people. Even our struggles can become openings through which wisdom, humility, and deeper trust emerge. The boundary between the ordinary and the holy begins to grow thin.

The promise of an open heaven is ultimately a promise of relationship. We are not abandoned in a disconnected universe. We live within a reality that is alive with meaning, presence, and love. The sacred is closer than we imagine, woven through every moment, quietly inviting us to participate in a life larger than our fears.

When our eyes begin to open, we discover that heaven has never been sealed shut. The Divine has always been reaching toward creation, and creation has always been held within the embrace of the Divine. What appears separate is already connected. What appears distant is already near. And what appears ordinary may be shining with more glory than we can yet see.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Open my eyes*
>
> Exhale: *Heaven is near*
