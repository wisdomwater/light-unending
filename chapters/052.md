## 52 — One Flock

> *“They will become one flock with one shepherd.”* — John 10:16

The human story is filled with divisions. We separate ourselves into nations, tribes, religions, cultures, ideologies, and countless other groups. Some of these distinctions are beautiful expressions of our diversity, yet they can also become walls that prevent us from recognizing one another as fellow travelers sharing the same sacred journey.

Beneath these differences lies a deeper unity. The same breath sustains us all. The same longing for love, belonging, meaning, and connection beats within every human heart. However different our stories may be, we are bound together by a common humanity and held within a Love far greater than our divisions.

The spiritual life gradually teaches us to see with wider eyes. We begin to notice traces of goodness in unexpected places. We discover wisdom among people we once considered strangers. We encounter compassion beyond the boundaries we had drawn. The circle of belonging grows larger than we imagined possible.

This does not mean that every path is identical or that all differences disappear. Rather, it means that divine love continually works toward reconciliation rather than separation. Love gathers. Love heals. Love creates bridges where fear builds walls. The movement of the heart of God is always toward wholeness.

Perhaps the deepest invitation is to trust that no one is beyond the reach of that love. The Shepherd continues calling across every boundary, every wound, every misunderstanding, and every distance we perceive. The call is gentle but persistent, drawing all creation toward a harmony not yet fully seen but already quietly unfolding.

When we listen for that voice, we find ourselves becoming more open, more compassionate, and more willing to embrace the mystery that we belong to one another. The journey toward God is also a journey toward each other, until at last we discover that the Love seeking us has been seeking everyone all along.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *We belong together*
>
> Exhale: *Love makes us one*
