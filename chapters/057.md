## 57 — The Fragrance of Love

> *"The house was filled with the fragrance of the ointment."* — John 12:3

Love often reveals itself through small and seemingly ordinary acts. A meal shared. A hand held. A listening ear. A gift given without expectation of return. These moments may appear insignificant when measured by the standards of efficiency or achievement, yet they carry a different kind of value. They touch something deeper than usefulness. They nourish the soul.

There is a way of living that constantly calculates cost, asks what is practical, and measures whether something is worth the expense. Such thinking has its place. Yet the deepest movements of the heart cannot always be reduced to a ledger. Love sometimes pours itself out freely simply because love is what it is.

Most of us can remember people whose kindness left a lingering fragrance in our lives. Perhaps they offered encouragement when we were discouraged, welcomed us when we felt like outsiders, or remained present during a difficult season. Years later, the details may fade, but the atmosphere of their love remains. Something beautiful was released into the world through them, and its scent still lingers.

The same is true of every act rooted in compassion. Love spreads beyond what we can measure. A gentle word may continue echoing in someone's heart long after we have forgotten speaking it. A small kindness may become a turning point in another person's story. We rarely see how far love travels once it leaves our hands.

The Divine Presence works much the same way. It does not force itself upon the world. It spreads quietly, gently, like fragrance filling a room. Wherever love is expressed, something of God becomes visible. Wherever compassion is offered, the air becomes a little lighter, the darkness a little thinner, and the world a little more whole.

Perhaps the invitation is not to accomplish something extraordinary today. Perhaps it is simply to become a vessel through which love can flow. The fragrance will take care of itself.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Let love flow through me*
>
> Exhale: *Fill the world with fragrance*
