## 16 — The Temple Within

> *"Destroy this temple, and in three days I will raise it up."* — John 2:19

There is a place within you that no circumstance can fully touch. Beneath your roles, your accomplishments, your fears, and even your changing thoughts, there is a deeper center where the sacred quietly dwells. We often spend much of our lives searching for God somewhere outside ourselves, while overlooking the holy ground already present within.

Many of us build our identities around structures that seem permanent. We rely upon success, certainty, approval, possessions, or carefully constructed self-images. Yet life has a way of shaking these foundations. Loss comes. Plans unravel. Seasons change. What once felt solid begins to crack. Though painful, these moments can become unexpected invitations into a deeper reality.

The spiritual journey is not about constructing a more impressive outer temple. It is about discovering the living presence that remains when lesser things fall away. What is most essential within us cannot be measured by achievement or diminished by failure. The Divine life is not built from human effort. It is a gift already waiting beneath the surface, quietly sustaining us even when we are unaware of it.

Sometimes transformation feels like a kind of dying. Old assumptions dissolve. Familiar identities no longer fit. The ways we once understood ourselves begin to give way to something larger and more spacious. Yet love is always at work beneath these endings. What appears to be loss may be making room for a deeper form of life to emerge.

The invitation is not to search farther but to listen more deeply. Beneath the noise of striving and beneath the fear of collapse, there is a sanctuary that remains. The sacred presence that fills the stars also fills the depths of your own being. You are not separate from the One you seek. The temple is closer than your next breath.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Holy Presence within*
>
> Exhale: *Awaken me to You*
