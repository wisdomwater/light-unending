## 68 — The Hidden Life of the Vine

> *"I am the vine. You are the branches."* — John 15:5

Much of life can feel like a struggle to become something. We strive to improve ourselves, prove our worth, overcome our weaknesses, and secure our place in the world. Yet beneath all this effort there remains a quieter truth: life itself is already flowing toward us.

A branch does not strain to produce its own life. It does not manufacture nourishment from within itself. Its strength comes from remaining connected to a source deeper than itself. Hidden beneath bark and leaf, an unseen current moves steadily through the vine into every branch.

So much of our spiritual exhaustion comes from forgetting this hidden connection. We imagine ourselves separate, isolated, responsible for carrying the whole weight of existence upon our shoulders. We forget that beneath every breath, every act of kindness, every longing for goodness, divine life is already present and at work.

The sacred is not far away, waiting to be discovered at the end of a difficult journey. The deeper reality is closer than our own thoughts. The love that sustains the stars and oceans is quietly sustaining us as well. Even when we cannot feel it, the current continues to flow.

Perhaps the invitation is not to become something new but to awaken to what has always been true. We belong to a greater Life. We are held within a Love larger than ourselves. The hidden life of the vine is already moving through every branch, drawing all things toward growth, healing, and wholeness.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Life flows through me*
>
> Exhale: *I am held in love*
