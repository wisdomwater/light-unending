## 40 — The Courage to Be Seen

> *"No man ever spoke like this man!"* — John 7:46

There are moments in life when something true touches us so deeply that we can no longer pretend not to have heard it. A conversation, a glimpse of beauty, an unexpected act of kindness, a quiet inner knowing—something awakens within us and asks for a response.

Yet responding to truth often requires courage.

Human beings naturally long for belonging. We want to be accepted by our families, communities, friends, and peers. Because of this, we sometimes hide what we genuinely feel, soften what we truly believe, or remain silent when our hearts are calling us to speak. Fear teaches us that safety lies in conformity. Love gently invites us into authenticity.

The spiritual path is filled with small moments of courage. Not the courage of grand gestures, but the courage of honesty. The courage to acknowledge what has touched us. The courage to admit that our hearts are changing. The courage to remain open when others would prefer certainty. The courage to follow light even when we cannot yet see where it leads.

Often the deepest transformations begin quietly. A person who once judged becomes more compassionate. A person driven by fear begins to trust. A person who felt disconnected starts to sense a presence woven through all things. These changes may be difficult to explain, but they are no less real. Something within recognizes life when it encounters it.

The good news is that we are never asked to walk this path alone. The same Presence that calls us forward also accompanies us. Divine love does not stand at a distance demanding bravery. It moves within us, strengthening the heart one small step at a time.

Perhaps the invitation today is simply to stop hiding from what is already stirring within you. Trust the quiet voice of love. Trust the light you have been given. The path unfolds not through certainty, but through the willingness to be seen by God and, little by little, to become your truest self.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Give me courage*
>
> Exhale: *To follow the light*
