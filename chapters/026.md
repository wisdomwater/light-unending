## 26 — The Harvest Already Growing

> *"Lift up your eyes and look at the fields, that they are white for harvest already."* — John 4:35

We often imagine that transformation is always somewhere in the future.

We tell ourselves that growth will come later, that healing will arrive someday, that the meaningful work of the soul has not yet begun. We wait for better circumstances, greater clarity, or a more favorable season. Meanwhile, life unfolds quietly around us, filled with opportunities we scarcely notice.

The invitation of the spiritual life is to open our eyes.

So much of God's work happens beneath the surface, hidden from immediate view. Seeds germinate in darkness long before they break through the soil. A heart softens through countless small moments of kindness. Wisdom forms through ordinary experiences. Love grows through daily acts of faithfulness that seem insignificant at the time. What appears unchanged may already be quietly transforming.

Because we cannot always see the process, we sometimes underestimate what grace is doing. We focus on what remains unfinished rather than on what is already growing. We measure progress by visible results while overlooking the deeper work taking place within the soul. Yet the Divine Gardener is often at work long before we recognize the fruit.

This is true not only in our own lives but in the lives of others. Every person we meet is carrying hidden seeds of possibility. A word of encouragement, a listening ear, a gesture of compassion, or a simple act of presence may become part of a harvest we never fully witness. Love leaves traces far beyond what we can measure.

The fields of life are fuller than they appear. Grace has been at work before we arrived, preparing hearts, healing wounds, and drawing people toward deeper life. We are not asked to control the harvest. We are only invited to participate in it, trusting that God is already doing more than we can see.

Lift up your eyes. Look again at your life, your relationships, and the world around you. Beneath the struggles and uncertainties, something beautiful is growing. The harvest has already begun.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Open my eyes*
>
> Exhale: *To what is growing*
