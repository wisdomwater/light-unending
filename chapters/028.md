## 28 — When Distance Disappears

> *"Your son lives."* — John 4:50

We often think of love as something limited by distance.

When those we care about are near, we feel connected. When they are far away, we feel the ache of separation. The same can be true in our spiritual lives. There are seasons when God feels close and immediate, and other seasons when the Divine seems distant, hidden behind silence, uncertainty, or the ordinary demands of life.

Yet distance is not always what it appears to be.

The deepest realities of life are not measured in miles or moments. Love reaches beyond physical presence. A kind word can remain with us for years. The memory of someone dear can warm the heart across continents. The bonds that matter most often operate beneath the surface of what we can see and touch.

The spiritual life gradually awakens us to a similar truth. The Presence of God is not confined by location. There is nowhere we must travel to find the One who already surrounds us. There is no barrier of space, circumstance, failure, or weakness that can place us beyond the reach of divine love. What appears distant may, in reality, be nearer than our own breath.

This realization begins to change the way we move through the world. We become less anxious when certainty is absent. We become less afraid when answers are delayed. We learn to trust that grace is working in places we cannot see and in lives we cannot fully understand. The reach of love is greater than our awareness of it.

Even when we feel alone, something deeper remains connected. Even when prayers seem unanswered, the current of divine life continues to flow. Even when the road ahead disappears into mystery, we are held within a Presence that never withdraws.

The journey of awakening is often the discovery that what seemed far away was never truly distant. The Love we sought across mountains, years, and circumstances has been quietly accompanying us all along. When the illusion of separation begins to fade, we find ourselves resting in a communion that has never been broken.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You are nearer than I know*
>
> Exhale: *Distance disappears in love*
