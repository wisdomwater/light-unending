## 82 — It Is Finished

> *"It is finished!"* — John 19:30

Many of us spend our lives trying to earn what has already been given.

We strive to become worthy. We try to prove ourselves. We imagine that peace waits somewhere ahead, after enough effort, achievement, improvement, or certainty. Yet the deepest truths of the spiritual life are often received rather than accomplished.

There comes a moment in every journey when striving begins to loosen its grip. We discover that love was present before our searching began. Grace was already at work long before we became aware of it. The ground beneath our feet was always being held.

What is finished is not the story of love but the illusion that love must be earned. What is finished is the exhausting belief that we stand apart from the Source of Life and must somehow find our own way back. The invitation has always been here.

The spiritual path is less about constructing a new self and more about releasing what obscures our true identity. Layer by layer, fear gives way to trust. Separation gives way to communion. Resistance gives way to surrender.

When we finally rest in what has already been given, we discover a strange freedom. Life becomes less about proving and more about participating. Less about achieving and more about awakening. The work of love continues, but it flows from rest rather than striving.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I release my striving*
>
> Exhale: *I rest in grace*
