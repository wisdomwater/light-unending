## 58 — Drawn Toward the Light

> *"And I, if I am lifted up from the earth, will draw all people to myself."* — John 12:32

There is a force at the heart of reality that is always drawing us toward life. Beneath our fears, beneath our striving, beneath the many stories we tell ourselves about who we are and where we belong, there remains a quiet invitation. We may resist it, ignore it, misunderstand it, or wander far from it, yet it continues calling to us all the same.

Many people imagine the spiritual journey as a search for God, as though the Divine were hidden somewhere far away. Yet often the deeper discovery is that we are the ones being sought. Long before we begin asking questions, longing for meaning, or reaching toward the mystery, Love is already reaching toward us.

The light does not shine only for the certain, the successful, or the spiritually accomplished. It shines for everyone. It shines upon the doubtful and the devoted, the wounded and the whole, the believer and the seeker alike. Like the sun rising over the world each morning, it offers itself freely without demanding worthiness in return.

Sometimes this drawing feels gentle and beautiful. Sometimes it feels unsettling. The light reveals what is true. It exposes the places where fear has built walls around the heart. It invites us to release old identities, old wounds, and old stories that no longer give life. Yet even this unveiling is an act of love. The purpose is never condemnation, but healing.

We see this movement everywhere. A longing for connection after loneliness. A desire to forgive after years of resentment. A sudden awareness of beauty that awakens gratitude. A quiet sense that there must be something more than fear and separation. These are often the first signs of the light gently drawing us home.

The invitation of the spiritual life is not to force ourselves toward God through effort alone. It is to become aware of the Love that is already drawing us. We do not create the movement. We awaken to it. The current has been carrying us all along.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You are drawing me*
>
> Exhale: *Into Your light*
