## 73 — The Spirit Who Guides Within

> *"The Spirit of truth... will guide you into all truth."* — John 16:13

There comes a point on every spiritual journey when borrowed answers are no longer enough. The words that once inspired us may still have value, but we begin to sense that truth must become something more than information. It must become lived experience. It must take root in the heart.

Many of us were taught to look for guidance primarily from outside ourselves—from teachers, traditions, books, or institutions. These can be beautiful gifts. Yet there is also a deeper wisdom that speaks from within. Beneath the noise of fear, beneath the endless stream of opinions and anxieties, there is a quiet presence gently drawing us toward what is real.

This guidance rarely arrives as certainty. More often it comes as a subtle nudge, a growing clarity, a sense of resonance that cannot be fully explained. It is the feeling that something is true not merely because it has been said, but because it awakens life within us. Love recognizes its own voice.

The journey into truth is not a journey into possessing all the answers. It is a journey into greater honesty, greater humility, and greater openness. The more we grow, the more we discover that truth is not a destination we conquer but a living reality that continually unfolds before us.

The Spirit does not force, shame, or manipulate. It invites. It illuminates. It patiently leads us one step at a time, always meeting us where we are. Even when we feel lost, the deeper current of divine love continues carrying us toward wholeness. We are not navigating life alone. The wisdom we seek is already nearer than our next breath.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Guide me into truth*
>
> Exhale: *I will listen within*
