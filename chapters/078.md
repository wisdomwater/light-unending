## 78 — Held in Divine Love

> *"That the love with which you loved me may be in them."* — John 17:26

Many of us spend our lives trying to become worthy of love. We work harder, achieve more, improve ourselves, and hide the parts we fear might be rejected. Somewhere along the way, we begin to believe that love is fragile—that it must be earned, protected, or constantly proven.

But divine love does not begin when we finally become acceptable. It begins before our first success and remains after our worst failure. It is older than our mistakes, deeper than our shame, and more enduring than our fears. It does not wait for perfection. It meets us exactly where we are.

The heart often imagines God as standing at a distance, evaluating our progress and measuring our worth. Yet the deeper spiritual life reveals something far more intimate. The Love we seek is already seeking us. The Presence we long for is already surrounding us. Like sunlight shining through an open window, grace arrives before we think to ask for it.

There are moments when this becomes unmistakably clear. Perhaps during a quiet walk, a conversation with a friend, the sight of a sunset, or a season of unexpected healing. For a brief instant, we remember that we are carried by something larger than ourselves. We are not holding life together by our own strength. We are being held.

To live from this awareness changes everything. Fear begins to loosen its grip. We become less concerned with proving ourselves and more willing to receive life as a gift. The heart relaxes. Compassion grows. Love that was once received begins to flow outward toward others.

The invitation is wonderfully simple: stop for a moment and allow yourself to be loved. Not someday. Not after you have changed. Not once you have figured everything out. Right here, in this breath, in this ordinary moment. The Love that sustains the stars is already closer than your next thought, holding you with a tenderness that never lets go.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I am held in love*
>
> Exhale: *Love holds all things*
