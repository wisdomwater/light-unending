## 87 — Blessed Are Those Who Cannot See

> *"Blessed are those who have not seen and have believed."* — John 20:29

Much of life unfolds in uncertainty. We rarely see the full path ahead. We make decisions without guarantees. We love without knowing how every story will end. We take the next step carrying questions that remain unanswered. The heart often longs for certainty, yet life invites us into something deeper than certainty.

Faith is not the absence of doubt. It is the willingness to remain open when complete proof is unavailable. It is the quiet trust that reality may be larger than what our eyes can presently perceive. Like a seed resting beneath the soil, much of what is most real is hidden from immediate view.

There are seasons when the Divine feels obvious and near. There are other seasons when the heavens seem silent and the signs we once relied upon disappear. Yet love does not cease simply because it cannot be measured. The sun continues shining even behind clouds. Presence remains present even when it is not felt.

The deepest movements of transformation often occur beneath the surface of awareness. A heart softens gradually. A wound heals quietly. Compassion grows unnoticed until one day we realize we are responding differently than before. We cannot always see grace at work, but that does not mean grace is absent.

Perhaps this blessing is not reserved for those who never question. Perhaps it belongs to all who continue walking despite their questions. It belongs to those who keep their hearts open to wonder, who choose love even when fear seems more reasonable, and who trust that the Light is present even when the path is dim.

We do not need to see everything in order to move forward. We do not need every answer before we begin. Sometimes the invitation is simply to take one more step, trusting that the One who called us into life is already waiting within the mystery ahead.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I trust the Light*
>
> Exhale: *Beyond what I see*
